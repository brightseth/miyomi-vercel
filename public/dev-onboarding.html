Architecture Decisions Made

1. Miyomi is an Oracle (Not a Trader)

This is critical: The 27 prototypes explored "trader" UX (P&L tracking, position management). Wrong direction.

What Miyomi Actually Is:

  • Influencer/commentator who highlights interesting markets
  • Revenue from attention (affiliates + subs), not trading profits
  • Content focus: "Here's a contrarian opportunity" not "I bought this"

2. Trainer as Validation Layer

AI generates → Trainer validates → Miyomi posts. Trainer's PM expertise is the secret sauce.

3. TikTok First, Others Second

Primary platform: TikTok (viral potential). Cross-post to Twitter/Farcaster. Don't optimize for YouTube yet.

4. Every Other Day Cadence

3-4 videos/week (not daily). Quality > quantity. Sustainable for trainer.

5. Affiliate Links Only (Phase 1)

Don't build premium tier subscriptions yet. Validate affiliate revenue first. Add subs later if traction.
