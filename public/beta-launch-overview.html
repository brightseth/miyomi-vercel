Launch Approaches

FULL STACK

  • Polished UI/UX

Aggressive timeline with lots of unknowns
PHASED
Launch basics in 2 weeks, polish over 4 weeks

Ship minimal viable beta, learn from real usage
  • Week 1: Live data + simple trainer
  • Week 2: First trade + video
  • Fastest path to learning
  • Lower commitment
